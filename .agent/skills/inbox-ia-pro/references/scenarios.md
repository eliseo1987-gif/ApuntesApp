# Email Scenario Examples

This reference provides concrete examples of emails and how the AI should reason about them within the "Inbox Inteligente IA®" ecosystem.

## Scenario 1: Supplier Price Update

- **Subject**: Actualización de Precios Mar-2026
- **Content**: "Estimados, informamos que a partir de mañana el perfil U de aluminio sube un 5% due to global raw material costs."
- **AI Reasoning**: "This email alerts a risk in costs. Suggesting update of stock pricing for quotations involving 'Perfil U Aluminio'."
- **Priority**: High (Urgency: Next Day)

## Scenario 2: Opportunity for New Sale

- **Subject**: Cotización Letreros de Seguridad
- **Content**: "Hola, necesito cotizar 50 letreros de seguridad en aluminio de 3mm para una obra en Paine. Es urgente."
- **AI Reasoning**: "Direct sales opportunity. Client needs high-quality signage. Suggesting material 'Aluminio 3mm' as per request and prior successful project #123."
- **Priority**: Critical

## Scenario 3: Bank Communication

- **Subject**: Cartola Mensual Marzo
- **Content**: "Su estado de cuenta ya está disponible en el portal del Banco BCI."
- **AI Reasoning**: "Financial routine. No immediate action required, but relevant for monthly balance."
- **Priority**: Low

## Scenario 4: Request for Information (RFI)

- **Subject**: Consulta sobre Tiempos de Entrega
- **Content**: "Quería saber cuánto se demoran en despachar a Santiago si pido hoy."
- **AI Reasoning**: "New lead inquiry. Performance status check required. Current production load: Medium. Response: 3-5 business days."
- **Priority**: Medium
